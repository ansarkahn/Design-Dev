Project Summary

For the Leaside Blvd new development project, we provided a full branding package and designed a brand-new website. Our aim was to create a cohesive and compelling online presence that reflects the allure of this exciting real estate venture.

Style Guide:

Our work on Leaside Blvd, a new development project, resulted in a website that improved usability, enhanced optimization for real estate visibility, increased speed, and introduced a clean and revised design and website structure. This creates a warm, inviting, and cohesive online presence for their project.



For this project, I placed a strong emphasis on crafting a visually striking and timeless platform. Understanding the client’s goals, I focused on usability, clear structure, and optimized performance to ensure the website not only stands out among competitors but also communicates the project’s message and offerings effectively.



As the designer for The Leaside Blvd Residences website, I focused on creating a clean, optimized, and engaging platform that highlights the project’s unique value proposition. The design was crafted to spark curiosity, capture interest, and help the development stand out in a competitive real estate market.



For The Leaside Blvd Residences project, I chose Webflow as the design and development platform to create a fully custom, dynamic, and responsive website. Leveraging Webflow’s flexibility, I crafted over 18 tailored pages that showcase the development’s unique selling points and key features. This approach allowed me to design a visually stunning, user-friendly platform that meets the client’s needs while standing out in the real estate market.
