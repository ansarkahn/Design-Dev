Project Summary

In the 2992 Sheppard development project, I applied a dedicated focus on creating an outstanding brand presence by crafting a complete and cohesive brand identity. With careful attention to detail, I captured the project’s essence in a visually captivating identity, highlighting the logo and its supporting elements to ensure a strong and memorable impression.

Style Guide:

I did more than just focus on visuals; I organized the content and created unique illustrations to ensure a cohesive and visually appealing brand that truly highlights the luxury and uniqueness of this project in Madrid.



I take great pride in delivering unparalleled results to my clients. For this project, I focused on crafting a visually striking and timeless platform. Recognizing its importance, I set clear goals and milestones to ensure an efficient process and impactful outcome. My unwavering determination was to create a design that not only stands out from the competition but also clearly communicates the client’s message and offerings.



The logo was inspired from the "zigzag" pattern thats portrait on the building across the stories.



Delft Blue was chosen as the primary color for the 2992 Sheppard brand because it adds sophistication and timeless charm, perfectly reflecting the theme “Elegance For All — Modern Community Living.”



The font Furore was selected because it complements the logo design while maintaining a modern, playful, and timeless aesthetic.

Our Process:

The project followed a structured process: beginning with a detailed project brief and research phase, moving into logo design, typography and color palette selection, and then developing the full brand application. Feedback and revisions refined the direction, leading to finalization of the identity. The brand was then applied consistently across media, launched and promoted strategically, and continues to be monitored and adapted for long-term impact.
